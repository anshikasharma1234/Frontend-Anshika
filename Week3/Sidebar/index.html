Welcome to Task 6: Responsive Sidebar

This sidebar demonstrates responsive design! On desktop (large screens), the sidebar stays fixed on the left side of the screen.

On mobile devices (screens smaller than 768px), the sidebar automatically transforms into a horizontal navigation bar at the top of the page.

Try this: Resize your browser window to see the sidebar change from vertical to horizontal layout! This is what "responsive design" means.

Hover over the navigation links to see the smooth transition effects. Notice how they slide slightly to the right and change color!
